Hi~ I am Tianyufei Zhou (周天羽菲), an MPhil student in [Data and Systems Engineering](https://www.dase.hku.hk/) at [HKU](https://www.hku.hk/), affiliated with the [NAISS Lab](https://naisslab.github.io/) and supervised by Prof. Shinan Liu.

## Research Interests

- Distributed Systems and Computer Systems  
- GPU Cluster Scheduling and Resource Management (HPC)  
- Systems for Machine Learning and Performance Optimization  


## News

- **Ongoing**: Working on synthetic ML job trace generation and evaluation for large-scale clusters.
- **Jan 2026**: Joined NAISS Lab as an MPhil student at HKU.
- **Jun 2025**: Received B.Eng. in Computer Science from [Sun Yat-sen University](https://www.sysu.edu.cn/), China.
